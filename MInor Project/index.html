About CareFinder

CareFinder started as a campus minor project to make hospital navigation less stressful for students and families. It has since evolved into a modular sample HMS frontend that demonstrates how hospitals can surface capacity information, coordinate ambulances, and keep staff workflows simple.

The design emphasises a calm, clinical aesthetic: soft blues, rounded edges, and typography that feels at home in a hospital environment. Every screen is optimised for both desktop and mobile, making it suitable for reception desks, triage nurses, and patients on their phones.

Our Mission

To reduce the anxiety of “where do we go now?” in medical emergencies by surfacing accurate, structured hospital information—beds, departments, and ambulance access—in one place. The project focuses on making it easy for hospital staff to keep this data current without disrupting their day.

Our Vision

A connected network of Indian hospitals and ambulances where patients, families, and administrators share the same live view of capacity and response times. CareFinder is intentionally lightweight so it can act as the UI layer on top of any robust HMS or EMR backend you choose.

Built around clinicians and patients

The UI is organised around real-world journeys: finding the right hospital, confirming capacity, and arranging safe transport.
